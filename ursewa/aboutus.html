WHAT ARE WE?

“ Urसेवा“ is a web portal which enables people to avail essential services without much hassle and improves the clientage of every person who earns on a daily basis. This portal has been currently made keeping in mind the ongoing pandemic condition and the dropping economy of every country.

OBJECTIVE

We all know that this time period of pandemic has effected every business in some way or the other. So many businesses have shut down, thousand of people have lost their jobs, hundreds of people are finding it difficult to run their home. The objective of creating this web portal is that people of all professions- be it a plumber, a carpenter, an electrician, a printer, a media person, a delivery man or any person who provides basic essential services as well as services which an individual or a company requires, pool in their services and help each other. We know that there are so many people who earn on a daily basis and run their home and these are the ones who have suffered the most. Its an alternative to lesser their burden and tension and help them earn by providing services to anyone and everyone who is in need of the service they provide, depending upon their preference of area where they would want to deliver service. This web portal also focuses on less people moving out of their homes or only those moving who are in need of.
